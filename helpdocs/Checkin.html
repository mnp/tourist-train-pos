If a Return train appears in red, that means its date is not the same as the Outbound train.

Checking in special customers

Checkin: Full Pmt -- This button is for when the customer is paying in full at checkin. Use this for ordinary reservations.

Checkin: Acct Rcvbl -- This button is for customers like Gray Line and Forest Adventures, which are on account. If you press it, they will OWE the amount shown due. You may also enter a payment in the box marked "Pmt" if they are paying a portion of their account at checkin time.
