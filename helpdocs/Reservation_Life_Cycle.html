A full reservation is created on the Reservation screen. A walkup reservation, with fewer details, is created on the Checkin screen.

Reservations are normally checked in when the passengers appear and are given tickets.

A reservation may be released, which means the seats are made available on the trains involved. This happens automatically at 15 minutes before the first train departs.

A reservation may be deleted any time, which also releases it. This also may happen automatically when the first train departs if the riders never checked in.
