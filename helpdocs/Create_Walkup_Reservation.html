Create a walkup customer by first checking one or two trains in the left two panes. (See help there).

Next, check off the passenger numbers.

Passenger name and state are optional. The name may be helpful if the passenger is not boarding immediately.

Finally check one of the reserve buttons. "Reserve" will create a Group or Rack reservation but not check it in. "Reserve and Checkin" will create the reservation and also check them in: it's for passengers who are boarding immediately.
